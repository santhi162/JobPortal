About Us - AVAKASH

Bridging Rural Talent with Local Opportunities

Our Vision

“Avakash” is a purpose-built rural employment portal designed to mitigate the disparity in access to skill development, job awareness, and employment opportunities in rural communities. It enables job seekers in underdeveloped areas to connect with local employers through a streamlined digital platform.

Objective

The primary objectives of Avakash are:

  • To connect local talent with localized job opportunities.
  • To support rural MSMEs (Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises) by linking them with skilled local workers.
  • To offer a simplified and intuitive user interface, ensuring inclusive accessibility even in low-digital-literacy regions.

Motivation

Rural areas often face challenges such as a lack of centralized job boards, absence of career counseling, and limited real-time market insights. Avakash leverages data-driven design and localized listings to provide clarity, confidence, and opportunities to rural job seekers. The inclusion of intelligent search filters and mobile-first design ensures efficient discovery even in bandwidth-constrained areas.

Technology Stack

Avakash is built using a combination of:

  • HTML5 for structure and semantic markup
  • CSS3 for responsive and modern visual design
  • JavaScript for client-side interactivity
  • WordPress as the content management platform for scalable and modular development

Scope & Future Enhancements

We aim to expand Avakash beyond rural sectors into semi-urban and urban territories. Upcoming features include:

  • Voice-based navigation for accessibility
  • Offline access and low-data UI mode
  • Promotion of remote work, self-employment, and hybrid part-time jobs to rural users
